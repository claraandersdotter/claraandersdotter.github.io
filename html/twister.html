A game

Type: Group work

When: 2023

Duration: Ten weeks, half time

Project scope: Creation of a game in collaboration with Prison Island

This project explored the dynamics of teamwork and collaboration within interaction design and the entertainment company Prison Island. The aim was to develop a new game cell, Twister with a twist, where teamwork and communication are essential.

The process

The project started with an ideation session, mainly brainstorming and braindrawing, to generate many ideas within a limited timeframe. After that, low-fidelity prototypes were made in different stages to develop the final idea further. Finally, a high-fidelity prototype was made to visualize the cell concept for Prison Island. The primary objective was to construct a visual representation that could be presented to the intended users for evaluation and feedback. The high-fidelity prototype was constructed using chipboards and joists. On the surface of the chipboard, 23 circles were marked out and then painted in the original Twister colors: red, green, blue, and yellow. Six holes were drilled around each circle, and behind each hole, an LED strip was positioned to illuminate based on an Arduino code.

The result

The developed cell Twister with a twist is an innovative take on the classic Twister game. In contrast to the traditional competitive nature of Twister, this version promotes teamwork and collaboration among players to collect points. To succeed in this game, participants must operate as a team to reach maximum points. Communication is essential since team members must synchronize their movements, predict each other's actions, and provide clear instructions to ensure efficient gameplay.
